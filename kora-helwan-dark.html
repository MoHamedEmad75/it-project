Football

Football is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players each with a spherical ball. With 250 million players in more than 200 countries, football is the most popular sport in the world. Football is played on a rectangular field with two goals at either side. The object of the game is to score goals by kicking the ball into the goal.

First: Top five leagues:

Premier league

The Premier League is a professional association football league in England and the highest level of the English football league system. Contested by 20 clubs, it operates on a system of promotion and relegation with the English Football League (EFL). Seasons usually run from August to May, with each team playing 38 matches.

La Liga

The Campeonato Nacional de Liga de Primera División, commonly known as the Primera División or La Liga and officially known as LaLiga EA Sports for sponsorship reasons, is a professional association football league in Spain and the highest level of the Spanish football league system. It is controlled by the Liga Nacional de Fútbol Profesional and it is contested by 20 teams over a 38-matchday period.

Seria A

The Italian league, known as Serie A for short and Serie A TIM for sponsorship reasons, is the highest-level professional football league in Italy and is considered one of the elite leagues in the world. It was first launched in 1898, it is contested by 20 teams over a 38-matchday period.

Bundesliga

The Bundesliga is the professional football league in Germany and is considered one of the world's elite leagues. It is considered the country's premier division, being the pinnacle of the German football league system. It is contested by 18 teams each team playes 34 matches.

Ligue 1

The French Ligue 1 is the professional football league competition in France. It was established in its current format in 2002 and is considered one of the world's elite leagues. The French league starts in August and ends in May. The number of clubs has been reduced from 20 to 18 starting with the 2023–24 season, with each team playing 34 matches, for a total of 306 matches per season.
